Personalised Learning On Demand



Getting help is easier than you think.

We are an online scheduling & services ordering system that enables users to register clients from various facets of industry who are available via our online platform. The online platform allows users to request a tutor in their desired subject area, to receive remedial training at a time most convenient.

As a privately contracted educator we noticed an opportunity in the area of specialized learning as a traditional learning and teaching experience has not sustained the pace of societal and cultural evolution. With persons seeking assistance they usually must do the majority of the work themselves. Tasks such as;
  1. Find the name of the best known teacher
  2. Learn of their most recent schedule
  3. Acquire a contact number
  4. Designate a location to conduct the session
  5. Arrange for a mutually advantageous date & time
These additional tasks make the process of hiring an individual incredibly exhausting. There is too much inefficiency in the entire process. The solution is an online marketplace designed to make the initial contact, and service delivery more efficient for those seeking assistance in any capacity. By providing an alternative to physical queues, customer satisfaction is maintained over time.
Athlene Learning offers private tutelage to those who are frustrated with the inadequacies of traditional teaching. Too often lessons' class sizes can be too big and the well meaning instructor is unable to provide the one on one instruction or remediation that the student desperately needs.
